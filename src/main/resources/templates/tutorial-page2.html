Step 2: Creating a New Snip

Now that you have opened the snipping tool, click the button labelled "New" to start creating a snip. After clicking the button, your screen will have a grey overlay. While the screen is grey, hold the left mouse button down on the top left area of what you want to snip and then drag your mouse to the bottom right of what you want to snip. This should create a rectangle around what you want to snip that makes it stop being grey. Let go of the right mouse button to create a snip of the normally colored area.
